Platform Services

Everything your fleet needs — dispatch, payments, analytics, and AI.

What We Offer

Built for scale, intelligence, and performance

Real-Time Dispatch

Live socket-powered location tracking with 2–5s driver updates. Driver assigned in under 5 seconds via weighted AI scoring.

AI Smart Matching

Proprietary algorithm weighs distance (40%), rating (25%), response time (20%), and history (15%) for optimal driver pairings.

Stripe Payments

Fare estimation, payment intents, instant receipts, and admin refunds. Supports dynamic surge pricing and VIP rates.

Analytics Dashboard

Revenue tracking, rides per day, peak-hour heatmaps, and top-driver rankings — all in a beautiful real-time admin panel.

Multi-Tenant SaaS

Fully isolated tenant architecture. Each fleet gets its own subdomain, branding, and data silo under one platform.

Safety & SOS

In-app SOS alerts pushed to admins and emergency contacts via FCM. AI chat moderation to keep rides safe and respectful.
