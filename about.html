Our Mission

SHARE-RIDE was built with one conviction: ride-hailing technology should be smart, affordable, and scalable for every fleet operator on the planet.

We combine real-time socket infrastructure, AI dispatch scoring, and multi-tenant SaaS architecture so fleets of any size can compete at the highest level — without building it all from scratch.

Our Journey

Q1 2025 — Concept & Research

Identified the gap in affordable AI dispatch tools for mid-sized fleet operators. Research phase begins.

Q2 2025 — First Build

Core platform launched: real-time socket infrastructure, driver matching algorithm v1, and Stripe billing integration.

Q3 2025 — Beta Launch

3 pilot fleet partners onboarded. 50,000+ ride data points collected to train smarter AI dispatch models.

Q4 2025 — SaaS Multi-Tenancy

Full multi-tenant architecture shipped with subdomain routing and white-label branding for fleet operators.

2026 — Scale & Expansion

Expanded to 3 countries. groq feedback engine, anomaly detection, and mobile apps (iOS/Android) released.
